XMUM Xplorer

XMUM Xplorer is a mobile application that helps students to track their time and share their campus life. It is a cross-platform app that is available on both Google Play and App Store.

  • Google Play
  • App Store

What is it all about?

Convenient Campus Life

Second Hand Market

Allowing students to sell and buy second-hand items, such as textbooks, clothes, and electronics.

Timetable

Providing students with a timetable that is automatically generated based on their course registration.

Campus Confessions

Allowing students to share their campus life anonymously and communicate with others.

The Best Platform for Campus Merchants

XMUM Xplorer provides a platform for campus merchants to promote their products and services. It is a great opportunity for merchants to reach out to students and promote their products and services.

Features

Merchants in campus and out of campus can promote their products and services on the platform. They can also manage their products and orders on the platform.

  • Merchant Background
  • Product Management
  • Voucher Management

Online Marketing

Can promote products and services on the platform.

Track Orders

Can manage products and orders on the platform.

Market analysis

Can view the sales and user data on the platform.

Full Settings

Can set the product price, discount, and other settings on the platform.
